Automatic Fallout Management

Automation can help to reduce the impact of fallouts by preventing fallouts from occurring and by resolving occurred fallouts. This web interface will provide the necessary means to view and manage fallouts and additional information supplied by the automated system.



Detected Fallouts

Manage and view fallouts picked up by the AFOM system.

Fallout Resolutions

Manage and view resolutions orchestrated by the AFOM system.

Statistics

View statistics and other data relating to the AFOM system.
